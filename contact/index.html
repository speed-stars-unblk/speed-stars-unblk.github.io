Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond within 24-48 hours for support inquiries. Bug reports may take longer as we investigate and fix issues. Suggestions are reviewed regularly but we may not respond to every submission individually.

Do I need to provide personal information to contact you?

No, all personal information is optional. However, providing an email address allows us to respond to your inquiry. We never share your information with third parties - see our Privacy Policy for details.

Why isn't the game working on my school computer?

Some schools have strict filters that may block our game. Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
  • Check if JavaScript is enabled in your browser settings
  • Try accessing the game during non-peak hours

If issues persist, contact us with details about your school's network.

Can I request specific games to be added?

Absolutely! We welcome game suggestions at ideas@speed-stars-unblk.github.io. While we can't promise to add every request, we consider all suggestions and prioritize popular, school-appropriate games.

Is Speed Stars Unblocked free to play?

Yes! Speed Stars Unblocked is completely free to play with no hidden costs or premium features. We believe in providing accessible gaming experiences for students and players everywhere.
